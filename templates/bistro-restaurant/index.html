Our story

A neighborhood table since 2014

We started with one idea: cook honest food with great ingredients and treat every guest like a friend. Ten years on, that hasn't changed.

Everything is made in-house, from the bread to the ice cream. Our menu follows the seasons and the market, so it's always a little different — and always fresh.
